# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 4, 2025
**Effective Date**: January 4, 2025

## 1. Introduction

LoLShorts ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application.

**By using LoLShorts, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information**
- Email address
- Username
- Password (encrypted, we never see your plain-text password)
- Subscription tier (FREE or PRO)

**Payment Information**
- Processed by Supabase/Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)
- Billing address
- Payment history
- Subscription status

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Application Usage Data**
- Features used within the application
- Session duration and frequency
- Button clicks and user interactions (anonymized)
- Error logs and crash reports

**Device Information**
- Operating system version (Windows 10/11)
- Hardware specifications (CPU, RAM, GPU)
- Application version
- Installation ID (random UUID, not tied to personal information)

**Gameplay Metadata**
- League of Legends summoner name (if connected)
- Game session timestamps
- Clip metadata (duration, event types, priority scores)
- **Note**: We do NOT collect or store your gameplay video recordings

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Your gameplay video recordings (stored locally on your device)
- ❌ Voice chat or text chat from League of Legends
- ❌ Account passwords for League of Legends or other services
- ❌ Browsing history outside the application
- ❌ Contacts or other personal files
- ❌ Location data beyond country-level for regional service

### 3.4 Security and Compliance
- Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions
- Enforce our Terms of Service

- Protect against security threats

### 3.5 What We DON'T Do
- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Use your information for targeted advertising

- ❌ Upload your gameplay recordings without explicit permission
- ❌ Monitor your activities outside the application

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your information based on:

- **Contractual Necessity**: To provide the services you've requested

### 5.1 Service Providers
We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our application:

**Supabase (Primary Infrastructure)**

- Data Shared: Account information, subscription status, application settings
- Location: United States
- Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

**Stripe (Payment Processing)**
- Purpose: Process subscription payments
- Data Shared: Payment information, billing address
- Location: United States

- Purpose: Video processing (runs locally on your device)
- Data Shared: None (all processing is local)

### 5.4 With Your Consent
We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Active Accounts
- Account information: Retained while your account is active
- Usage data: Aggregated and retained indefinitely for analytics
- Error logs: Retained for 90 days

### 6.2 Closed Accounts
When you close your account:
- Account data is deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized usage statistics may be retained
- Legal and financial records retained as required by law

### 6.3 Local Data
- Video recordings are stored on your device
- We do not automatically delete local files when you uninstall
- You must manually delete recordings from your device

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security practices:
- **Encryption in Transit**: All data transmitted using TLS/SSL
- **Encryption at Rest**: Database encryption via Supabase
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access for our team
- **Authentication**: Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Monitoring**: Automated security monitoring and alerts

### 7.2 Local Data Security
- Video recordings are stored on your device
- We recommend encrypting your hard drive (Windows BitLocker)
- Protect your device with strong passwords

### 7.3 Limitations
No security system is perfect. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights

### 8.1 Rights for All Users
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal information
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Opt-Out**: Unsubscribe from marketing emails

### 8.2 Additional Rights (GDPR - EEA Users)

- **Restrict Processing**: Request limitation of data processing
- **Data Portability**: Receive data in structured, commonly used format
- **Right to Lodge Complaint**: File complaint with data protection authority

### 8.3 Additional Rights (CCPA - California Users)
- **Know**: Know what personal information is collected
- **Delete**: Request deletion of personal information
- **Opt-Out of Sale**: We do NOT sell your information
- **Non-Discrimination**: Equal service regardless of exercising rights

### 8.4 Exercising Your Rights
To exercise these rights:
- **Email**: privacy@lolshorts.com
- **In-App**: Settings → Privacy → Data Requests
- **Response Time**: We respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA)
